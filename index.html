Our Services

Hotels

We offer our customers, a highly furnished, 7 star Luxury Rooms in our Hotel. The Hotel Castle is located near Juhu Beach, just a 10-minute walk from the Holy Cross Church. Built in 1956 it has old school charm with high, crown-molded ceilings. A 2012 renovation brought all rooms and services up to modern day scratch and guestrooms come equipped with free Wi-Fi and all the usual amenities required for a comfortable stay.

Fun Activities

Whatever kind of outdoor activity you’re into, we have it all: whale-watching, fishing, walking, swimming. Or maybe you’d prefer something with a slightly higher thrill factor? So-called ‘extreme sports’, such as bungee jumping and tandem skydiving. All of the above mentioned activites are made available at a very customer friendly rate.

Car Rentals

Ownership is a deal. Similarly, our customers satisfaction is our deal. Taking that into account, we also offer our guests to rent cars to visit their places of interest, at an affordable price. This is a step to make your travelling hassle free. Owing to the COVID - 19 scenario, we take utmost care and keep sanitizing the cars every now and then. Your safety, is our Priority.
